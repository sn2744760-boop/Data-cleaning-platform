Health Score Methodology

A composite metric out of 100% evaluating data quality across five key dimensions:

  • −
    Missing Values (Max −30 pts): Deducts 2 pts per 1% null cell density.
  • −
    Duplicate Rows (Max −20 pts): Proportional to duplicate row ratio.
  • −
    Outlier Values (Max −15 pts): Statistical anomalies via IQR method.
  • −
    Email Validity (Max −10 pts): Invalid or malformed email addresses.
  • −
    Type Incoherency (Max −10 pts): Currency/numbers stored as strings.

Optimizing your dataset addresses all issues above, moving the score toward 100%.
